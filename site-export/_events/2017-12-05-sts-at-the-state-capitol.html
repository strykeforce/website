Stryke Force Robotics team 2767 will be traveling with their World Championship robot to the Lansing this week for the Student Technology Showcase hosted by MACUL. Stryke Force members will be in their pit with the robot from 11:30am - 1pm in the South Wing of the Michigan State Capitol Building at Table 10.  

The showcase is a day-long event held in the Michigan State Capitol Building to allow lawmakers, business leaders and other citizens to see first-hand how technology is used in classrooms across Michigan.  K-12 student teams representing their schools from around the state will demonstrate best practices of how technology can enhance teaching and learning for the success of Michigan students. 

 

We hope to see you there!
